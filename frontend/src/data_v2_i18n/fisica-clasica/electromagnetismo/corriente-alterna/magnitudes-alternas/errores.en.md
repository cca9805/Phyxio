## Conceptual errors


### Error 1: assuming peak and RMS are equivalent
Why it seems correct:
Both are expressed in the same electrical units and often appear side by side.

Detection signal:
Peak value is directly used in energetic conclusions without conversion.

Contrast mini-example:
A signal may have large peak while useful active transfer is determined by RMS and phase context.

Conceptual correction:
Peak describes extrema; RMS describes energetic equivalence.
