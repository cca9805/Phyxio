# Direct-current circuits

## General idea of the topic
This block organises the systematic analysis of direct-current networks,
from the reduction of series and parallel configurations to the general
node and mesh equations derived from Kirchhoff's laws.

The objective is for the student to master a structured method for solving
any DC network while verifying charge conservation at every node and
energy conservation in every closed loop.

## What you will truly understand
- How to collapse series and parallel networks without losing physical interpretation.
- How to apply KCL and KVL with a consistent sign convention.
- How to distribute current, voltage and power by branch.

## Recommended learning route
1. Resistors in series.
2. Resistors in parallel.
3. Kirchhoff's laws.

## Physical criterion you must acquire
Always verify that node and mesh balances close before accepting the numerical result.

## Connection with real problems
Models everything from dividers and sensors to distribution panels and fault diagnosis.

## Final goal of the topic
Solve direct-current networks systematically, verifying conservation of charge and energy.
