# Common mistakes in gravitational potential energy

## Conceptual errors

### Error 1: treating [[U]] as always positive
Por que parece correcto: students often learn energy as a nonnegative scalar without reference discussion.
Senal de deteccion: [[U]] appears positive in a bound state while infinity reference is implied.
Mini-ejemplo de contraste: under infinity reference, low orbit configurations require negative [[U]].
Correccion conceptual: declare the reference first and keep it consistent across all steps.

## Model errors

### Error 2: using local approximation outside its domain
Por que parece correcto: local form looks shorter and familiar from introductory classes.
Senal de deteccion: [[h]] and [[g]] are used for radial changes comparable to planetary radius.
Mini-ejemplo de contraste: if h/r is not small, local approximation distorts [[DeltaU]] significantly.
Correccion conceptual: switch to the full radial model using [[ri]] and [[rf]].

## Mathematical errors

### Error 3: swapping initial and final states in [[DeltaU]]
Por que parece correcto: substitution is done before physically mapping process direction.
Senal de deteccion: computed sign contradicts outward or inward motion narrative.
Mini-ejemplo de contraste: outward transfer should yield positive [[DeltaU]] in standard reference.
Correccion conceptual: write state logic first, then substitute values.

### Error 4: missing the sign link between [[Wg]] and [[DeltaU]]
Por que parece correcto: mixed sign conventions from force and energy chapters.
Senal de deteccion: [[Wg]] and [[DeltaU]] are reported with the same sign in conservative motion.
Mini-ejemplo de contraste: gravitational work must oppose potential-energy change in this regime.
Correccion conceptual: enforce a conservation cross-check before final answer.

## Interpretation errors

### Error 5: finishing with numbers but no physical reading
Por que parece correcto: algebraic sequence seems complete and clean.
Senal de deteccion: no statement about what signs imply for motion or binding.
Mini-ejemplo de contraste: similar numeric magnitudes may represent opposite physical scenarios.
Correccion conceptual: close with one sentence on state, transfer direction, and dynamical consequence.

## Quick self-control rule

Define [[ri]] and [[rf]] before any arithmetic. If the process is conservative, verify opposite signs between [[Wg]] and [[DeltaU]]. If the scale is not local, avoid relying on [[h]] and [[g]] as the main model. If the final line does not interpret [[E]] or transfer meaning, the solution is still incomplete.
