# Fundamentals

## General idea of the topic

This topic builds the basic language of waves. It defines what a wave disturbance is, how it is represented mathematically, and which quantities describe it.

## What you will truly understand

You will understand the difference between amplitude, phase, frequency, period, wavelength, and speed. You will also see why the wave equation expresses a physical relation between spatial variation and temporal variation.

## Recommended learning route

Start with the concept of a wave. Continue with the wave equation and wave quantities. Then classify waves according to their nature and according to the direction of the disturbance.

## Physical criterion you must acquire

You should learn to identify which physical variable oscillates and which relation makes it propagate. Before using formulas, you must know whether the problem concerns a string, a pressure, a field, or a deformation.

## Connection with real problems

The fundamentals are used in sound, optics, vibrations, seismic waves, communications, materials, and any system where a disturbance propagates.

## Final goal of the topic

The final goal is to have a common physical and mathematical vocabulary for interpreting any later wave topic without relying on isolated recipes.
