# Waves

## General Idea Of The Topic

Waves describe how a disturbance propagates through space and time while carrying energy and information. This block unifies different situations, such as strings, sound, fluids, solids, and wave phenomena, under the same way of reasoning.

## What You Will Truly Understand

You will understand that a wave is not an object traveling through space, but a pattern of change that is transmitted. You will learn to separate motion of the medium, propagation of the disturbance, phase, frequency, wavelength, speed, and intensity.

## Recommended Teaching Path

Start with fundamentals, where the common vocabulary is built. Continue with mechanical waves to see the role of the medium. Then study wave phenomena, standing waves, energy, and sound as progressively more concrete applications.

## Physical Criterion To Acquire

The essential criterion is to identify which quantity oscillates, which medium or field allows propagation, which condition sets the speed, and what information phase provides. Without that criterion, formulas are applied mechanically and contexts are confused.

## Connection With Real Problems

Waves appear in sound, vibrations, ultrasound, telecommunications, seismology, optics, musical instruments, and material diagnosis. The block prepares you to interpret measurements, patterns, resonances, and energy transfer.

## Final Goal Of The Topic

By the end, you should be able to read a wave situation, choose the relevant quantities, justify the physical model, and solve problems without losing the interpretation of what is propagating.
